The Challenge

Our client, a tier-1 logistics provider operating across 40 countries, faced escalating operational costs due to inefficient route planning and reactive maintenance protocols. Their legacy systems were fragmented, creating data silos that prevented real-time visibility into their supply chain.

Critical delays were often identified only after they occurred, leading to SLA breaches and customer dissatisfaction. They needed a unified, predictive system capable of processing terabytes of sensor data daily.

psychology

The Solution

Cervus Labs engineered a bespoke "Digital Twin" of their entire logistics network. Utilizing a microservices architecture on AWS, we ingested data streams from IoT devices on shipping containers, vehicles, and warehouse hubs.
